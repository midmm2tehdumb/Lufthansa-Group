Cabin & Seating Configuration (European)

The A319, the smallest of the A320 family, utilizes Lufthansa's flexible European seating for efficient regional operations.

  • **Total Seats:** ~138 - 144 passengers
  • **Business Class:** Variable pitch, typically 15-25 seats (Economy seats with middle seat blocked).
