1. Safety and Incident Reporting

1.1 Incident Reporting Mandate

All pilots MUST report any occurrence that affects flight safety. Reports are mandatory for:

  • Hard Landings: Any touchdown exceeding 600 fpm or 2.2G.
  • Tail Strikes/Wing Strikes: Immediate suspension of flight.
  • Unstable Approach: Failure to meet criteria at 1,000ft AGL (IMC) or 500ft AGL (VMC).
  • Fuel Emergencies: Landing with less than 30 minutes of reserve fuel.

Advisory: Failure to report incidents is grounds for immediate rank demotion.

2.2 Visibility & Hazards

Thunderstorms: Do not fly within 20nm of red/magenta cells. If caught in severe turbulence, maintain VA (Maneuvering Speed) and do not fight the autopilot unless it disconnects.

4.1 Airbus A320 Family (A319/320/321)

Phase: Takeoff & Climb

  • Thrust: FLX/MCT (Normal) or TOGA (Short/Wet).
  • Rotation: 145 KIAS. Pitch to 15° (max 18°).
  • Climb: 250 KIAS below 10,000ft. Cruise Mach: 0.78.

Phase: Approach & Landing

  • Flaps 1: 200 KIAS. Flaps 2: 180 KIAS.
  • Configuration: Gear Down by 2,000ft AGL.
  • Vref: 135-140 KIAS (Flaps FULL).
  • Touchdown: Target -200fpm. Retard thrust at 20ft.

4.8 Bombardier CRJ-700

Operational Advisories

Warning: The CRJ-700 has no leading-edge slats. High angles of attack at low speeds will result in an immediate aerodynamic stall.

Phase: Takeoff & Landing

  • V1/VR: 135 KIAS. V2: 145 KIAS.
  • Landing: Flaps 45. Vref: 130 KIAS.
  • Advisory: Do not flare excessively. The CRJ should be "flown onto the runway" with a slight pitch increase (3°) at 10ft.

4.7 Boeing 747-8i

Phase: Takeoff & Cruise

  • Rotation: 165 KIAS. Pitch: 12.5°.
  • Cruise: Mach 0.85 - 0.86.

Phase: Landing

  • Vref: 155 KIAS (Flaps 30).
  • Advisory: Account for "Main Gear Steering" during taxi. Do not exceed 10kts in turns to avoid scrubbing tires.
