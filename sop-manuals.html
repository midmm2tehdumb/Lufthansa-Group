Standard Operating Procedures

GENERAL PROCEDURES
AIRCRAFT SPECIFICS
View Original Document ↗

1. Safety and Incident Reporting

1.1 Mandatory Occurrence Reporting (MOR)

All pilots are mandated to report safety-related incidents via the Discord #incident-reports channel within 24 hours.

  • Hard Landings: Exceeding 600 fpm or 2.2G.
  • Airframe Damage: Tail/Wing/Engine strikes during any phase.
  • Fuel: Landing with < 30m reserve or < 5% total fuel.
  • Incursions: Runway incursions or loss of separation (< 1000ft vertical, < 3nm lateral).

1.2 Unstable Approach Criteria

A "Go-Around" is MANDATORY if the following are not met by 1,000ft (IMC) or 500ft (VMC):

  • Planned Landing Configuration (Flaps/Gear).
  • Speed within -5 to +10 kts of Vref.
  • Vertical Speed < 1,000 fpm.
  • Engines stabilized at approach thrust.

1.3 Ground Operations & Safety

Taxi speed must not exceed 25 kts on straightaways and 10 kts in turns. Pilots must use exterior lighting as follows:

  • Beacons: On before engine start, off after shutdown.
  • Taxis: On when moving on ground.
  • Strobes: On when entering the active runway.
  • Landings: On below 10,000ft AGL.

1.4 Professionalism & Conduct

Pilots must use official "DLH" callsigns. Harassment or intentionally disrupting other GeoFS players is grounds for immediate termination.

2.2 Severe Weather Avoidance

  • Thunderstorms: 20nm clearance from red/magenta cells.
  • Severe Turbulence: Reduce to VA (Maneuvering Speed) and set ignition to CONT/ON.
  • Low Visibility: CAT II/III ILS required for visibility < 550m.

3. Speed & Flight Management

3.1 Speed Schedule

  • Below 10,000ft: 250 KIAS Max.
  • Below 3,000ft: 220 KIAS Max.
  • Approach: Clean (210), Flaps 1 (190), Flaps 2 (170).

3.2 Altitude & Navigation

RVSM logic applies above FL290. Semicircular Rule:

  • 000-179°: Odd FL (310, 330, 350).
  • 180-359°: Even FL (320, 340, 360).

4.1 Airbus A320/A321

Takeoff & Climb

  • Rotation (Vr): 145 KIAS. 15° Pitch.
  • Thrust: FLX/MCT. TOGA only for short/contaminated runways.

Landing

  • Vref: 135-140 KIAS (Flaps Full).
  • Flare: Start at 20ft. Retard thrust at "Retard" callout.

4.2 Airbus A350-900

Performance

  • Rotation: 155 KIAS.
  • Cruise: Mach 0.85. Max FL431.

Landing Configuration

  • Vref: 145 KIAS.
  • Note: 2-3° landing pitch. Avoid over-flaring (tail strike risk).

4.7 Boeing 747-8i

Operations

  • Rotation: 165 KIAS. 12.5° Pitch.
  • Vref: 155 KIAS (Flaps 30).
  • Taxi: Main gear steering active. Max 10 kts in turns.

4.8 Bombardier CRJ-700

Stall Warning

No leading-edge slats. High Alpha = Instant Stall. Maintain speed in turns.

Landing

  • Vref: 130 KIAS.
  • Pitch: Do not exceed 3° pitch at 10ft. "Fly" it onto the deck.
