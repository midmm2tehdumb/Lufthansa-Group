About WestJet Virtual

WestJet Airlines (GeoFS) is a community-based virtual airline inspired by the real-world WestJet. We operate domestic, international, and long-haul routes across North America, Europe, and Asia with a modern fleet of Boeing aircraft.

Our Mission

To bring realism, professionalism, and community engagement to the GeoFS flight simulator while still allowing freedom. We aim to replicate real airline operations while keeping things fun and accessible for everyone.
